Welcome to Fred's Fantastic Adventure!

Our friend, Fred, is an IRS auditor. He wakes up without snoozing at 7:30 am, walks the 8 minutes and 30 seconds it takes to get to work, eats the same peanut butter and jelly sandwich for lunch, then leaves his cubicled office at precisely 5:00 pm. Fred shies away from all deviation and distraction...except for tonight...

As Fred lays peacefully in his twin bed, he hears a burglar prying open his bedroom window. In his bleary-eyed, shocked state, he realizes he must do something quickly. What does Fred do?

  1. Of course, Fred avoids all confrontation, so he decides to quietly slip into his closet.
  2. He remembers the pepper spray that his mother gave him for Christmas, and grabs it from under his bed.
  3. He decides this must be a dream, and hums himself to sleep.
