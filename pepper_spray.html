Continuing Fred's Fantastic Adventure...

With pepper spray in hand, he anxiously awaits the burglar that is furtively climbing through his window. He grips the can and sprays the liquid pepper into his own face! He screams like a banshee!

Fred cries himself to sleep, and in the morning, he wakes up and starts anew. On his 8 minute and 30 second walk to work, he encounters a worm hole swirling in the crosswalk. What does Fred do?

  • Shaken by the past night's encounter with death, Fred trips on his own feet and falls face first into the worm hole
  • Wary of deviating from his normal routine, he walks a wide radius around the worm hole, safely making it to the other side of the street.
